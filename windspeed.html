Windspeed

Wind speed does not appear correlated with latitude. Wind speed appears to group around lower speeds.

It may be worth investigating a trend in the wind data over a longer period of time across the latitudes.

This may yield trends in seasonal wind speeds in certain areas.

Certainly it could be expected that wind speeds would show higher maximum values during hurricane season, but further research is needed to determine what season may hold the lowest wind speeds on average.
