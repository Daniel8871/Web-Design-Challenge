Cloudiness

Plot indicates that cloud coverage tends to group in the extremes of 100% or 0%, possibly due to methods of reporting the data.

Unlike the other metrics reported on this site, cloud coverage changes within a single day can be both drastic, and disposed to human interpretation.

In areas that may not have resources to use technology to calculate cloud coverage, the coverage in the area may rely entirely on estimates, and could account for the strong clustering trend observed in this data set.
